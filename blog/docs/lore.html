With the introduction of Scarcity Island, a burgeoning story has began to form in the CV-Metaverse.

Lit by Bitpixi and brought into reality by the CV dev team, 'Scarcity-Discoveries' have ignited a fire in our hearts and minds.

Since their reveal, a dauntless core of of creatives have banded (loosely) together to keep our "cosa nostra" rolling.

Below you will find a breakdown of SOME of these participants and SOME of their contributions towards the ever-developing CV Meta Story.

The Heresy of Hagarthi the Immaterial

An ascetic gnome monk retreated to a mountain cave for a decade of fasting in silence and was never seen again. A search party from the monastery was sent on the 11th year and found only Hagarthi's pointy hat sitting pristinely in the middle of the cave. The hat began emanating vibrations in the 11th dimension once placed in the Vault of Reliquaries back in the monastery, causing the same recurring dream for all the monks in the monastery.

In this dream Hagarthi appeared and shared a revelation: After achieving a transcendent state in meditation, Hagarthi's body de-materialized and regained consciousness after meditation as the hat. Hagarthi had glimpsed the true nature of Gnomish reality; the physical body is but a reflection of the true gnome which always resides in a pointy hat (the pointy shape serving as a conduit between the false reality and the true hativerse).

This revelation was rejected as a heinous heresy of the 12th degree at the third council of Hrghrvtfffan and Hagarthi's hat was removed to the crypts. Hagarthi re-materialized from the hat (in the eyes of some observers proving the heresy correct) and promptly marched the hat back to the Vault of Reliquaries whence Hagarthi de-materialized again and the hat has remained ever since. Adherents to the Heresy of Hagarthi left behind a scroll which seems to imply that the pointy hats found around Scarcity are actually the missing gnomes in a state of suspended potentiality, perhaps waiting for the vibration of the perfect frequency to return.
